<h2>Pingtung (Taiwan) and Gangwon-do (South Korea)</h2>
<h4>Fieldwork associated with '<a href="https://jimarcor.github.io/research/">The Global Cyperaceae Database (GCypD)</a>' project (PI: Pedro Jiménez-Mejías)</h4>

I visited some herbaria in Taipei (TAIF) and South Korea (KNA), and collected sedges. The trip lasted from **October 8<sup>th</sup>** to **November 1<sup>st</sup> 2025**.
